How It Works

There is no maximum or minimum guest count. Every event has a $500 minimum spend — typically about 10 adults at our base rate.

Our base rate is $50 per adult and $25 per child under 12, with a $500 minimum spend for all events. Each guest receives salad, fried rice, hibachi vegetables, and two protein choices (chicken, steak, shrimp, salmon or tofu).

Premium protein upgrades and add-ons may apply — see our menu & pricing page for details. *Price may vary by location. Suggested gratuity of 20% is not included.

Hibachi2Party accepts cash only. There is no deposit required — full payment is due on the day of your event.
