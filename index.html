How It Works

Private hibachi chef at your home

From online booking to live teppanyaki in your backyard — here is how mobile hibachi catering works with Hibachi2Party.

1

Book online

Choose your city, pick a date, and reserve your private hibachi chef in minutes.

2

We bring chef & grill

Our team arrives with the mobile hibachi grill, fresh ingredients, and everything needed to cook on-site.

3

You set the table

You provide tables, chairs, plates and utensils. Each guest gets salad, fried rice, vegetables and 2 proteins.

4

Enjoy the show

Watch fire, knife tricks and laughter while your chef cooks a full hibachi meal live at your party.
